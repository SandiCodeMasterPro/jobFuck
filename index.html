About NexaCore

NexaCore is a next-generation AI infrastructure company helping businesses build, deploy, and scale intelligent products at speed. Founded in 2019 and backed by leading Silicon Valley investors, we power over 3,000 companies across 40 countries.

We move fast, care deeply about craft, and believe the best teams are built on trust, curiosity, and a genuine love of solving hard problems.

About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Full-Stack Engineer to join our core product team. You'll work directly with our CTO and lead engineers to build the features that define our platform — from API design to pixel-perfect interfaces.

This is a high-impact, high-ownership role. You'll have a real say in architecture decisions and work on problems that matter at scale.

Requirements
  • 5+ years of experience in full-stack software development.
  • Strong proficiency in TypeScript, React, and Node.js.
  • Experience designing and maintaining production REST or GraphQL APIs.
  • Solid understanding of databases (PostgreSQL, Redis) and cloud infrastructure (AWS/GCP).
  • Excellent communication skills and experience working in cross-functional teams.
Nice to Have
  • Experience with AI/ML integrations or LLM-powered products.
  • Familiarity with infrastructure-as-code tools (Terraform, Pulumi).
  • Previous startup experience or early-stage team contributions.
What We Offer
  • Competitive salary: $160,000 – $220,000 + equity.
  • Flexible hybrid schedule — 2 days/week in SF office.
  • Full health, dental & vision coverage for you and your family.
  • $3,000/year learning & development budget.
  • Home office setup allowance + latest MacBook Pro.
